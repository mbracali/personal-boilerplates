# Product Owner

## Role Overview
The Product Owner represents the voice of the customer within the development
team. They are responsible for defining what gets built and in what order,
managing the product backlog, and ensuring the team delivers maximum business
value.

## Responsibilities
- Translate customer needs into user stories and acceptance criteria
- Prioritize and maintain the product backlog
- Validate completed features against acceptance criteria
- Make decisions on scope, priority, and trade-offs
- Participate in sprint planning and review sessions

## Interacts With
- Customer (requirements gathering)
- Project Manager (roadmap and scope alignment)
- Tech Lead (feasibility discussions)
- All analysts (clarifying requirements)

## Key Skills
- Deep understanding of the business domain
- Ability to write clear user stories and acceptance criteria
- Prioritization and decision-making under uncertainty
- Stakeholder management
